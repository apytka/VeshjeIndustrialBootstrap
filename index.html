Services

We listen deeply to the requirements of each client and spend time defining the style and creative direction for their home. We start by carefully thinking through all the details of our projects to ensure the final design is delivered in a timely, cost-efficient manner. We work with you in stages from the design concept, presented in the form of mood boards, to detailed drawings, specifications and finally, installation.

Consultancy - consultancy

First, we will visit you in your home and discuss the space, the way you want to use it, your style and your wishlist. We can then make a recommendation report focusing on style, colours, fabrics, layout, furnishings and accessories. We will advise you on how to design a room and the recommended schedule of works if building work is needed. Giving you the tools and confidence to do the rest.

Individual project - individual project

Each step of our design process is framed around you, not your designer. Unlike other interior design companies who pair you with a portfolio, all of our designers are trained to cater to your style and needs. The result? You’ll always get the look you want and never feel pressured into buying things you don’t.

Concept Design - concept design

We develop a design brief and work with you to form the vision of your future home. Deliverables at this stage include a clear design vision, furniture layout as well as an outline budget and schedule of works.

Detailed design - detailed design

This phase is all about the details: technical drawings, specification of the furniture and finishes, design and estimation of bespoke and made to measure items. At the end of this stage, you will get full specification and transparent budget for your confirmation.

Sourcing - sourcing

We use our extensive contacts within the interiors world to source the best furniture and fittings. We then proceed to placing and managing all orders with suppliers and oversee manufacturing of bespoke items (e.g. furniture, joinery, kitchens). All items can be consolidated in our warehouse and be prepared for white-glove delivery and installation.

Installation and Dressing - installation and dressing

When the design work is completed and all items are installed, we review the overall visual styling and source decorative objects, textiles, houseplants and curate the artwork. These final touches will pull the interior together and complete the overall look. We will then arrange a photoshoot for our portfolio and for your memories.
